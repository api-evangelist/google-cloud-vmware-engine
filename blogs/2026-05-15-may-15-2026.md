Announcement ve1 hardware End-of-Life (EoL) migration guide: You can now refer to the public documentation to migrate workloads from retiring ve1 hardware . First-generation ve1 bare metal nodes are reaching the end of their useful life on a rolling basis. When your hardware is scheduled for retirement, you receive an EoL notification containing timelines and instructions to migrate your clusters.
